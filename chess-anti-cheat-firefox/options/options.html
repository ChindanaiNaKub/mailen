About

Chess.com Opponent Risk Score Extension v1.0.0

This extension helps detect potential cheaters by analyzing opponent statistics using Chess.com's public API. The risk score is calculated based on win rates, accuracy, and account age.

⚠️ Note: A high risk score does not definitively indicate cheating. Use this as one of many factors in your assessment.
